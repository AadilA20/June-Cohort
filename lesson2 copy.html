Lovely Brownie Recipe

This page will teach you how to make proper irresistable brownies
Make sure you read the steps carefully

What you need

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Powder
  • Teaspoon Salt
  • Walnut Halves

    • Nitty Gritty

    Cooking time is approx 20 minutes.
    Bear in mind that you will need 10 minutes of prep time.

    1. Step 1
      • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
      • Grease in a 9x13 inch pan
    2. Step 2
      • Add into a large bowl and mix until combined
        • 2 cups of white sugar
        • 1 and 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
        • 1 cup of melted butter
        • 4 eggs
        • 1/2 cups of cocoa powder
        • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
        • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
        • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
    3. Step 3
      • Spread the batter into the prepared pan
      • Decorate with walnut halves
    4. Step 4
      • Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges gave started to pull away from the sides of the pan
      • This will take around 20-30 minutes
      • Cool before slicing into squares
